Unordered lists

Syntax


* Line 1
* Line 2
** Line 3
** Line 4
*** Line 5
*** Line 6
** Line 7
*Line 8


Result

  • Line 1
  • Line 2
    • Line 3
    • Line 4
      • Line 5
      • Line 6
    • Line 7
  • Line 8

Ordered lists

Syntax


# Line 1
# Line 2
## Line 3
## Line 4
### Line 5
### Line 6
## Line 7
# Line 8


Result

  1. Line 1
  2. Line 2
    1. Line 3
    2. Line 4
      1. Line 5
      2. Line 6
    3. Line 7
  3. Line 8

Syntax

# Line 2

# Line 8
